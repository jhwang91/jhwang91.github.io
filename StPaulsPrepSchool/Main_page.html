SPPS stands on a mission of independent learning. By providing a rigorous curriculum in a caring atmosphere touting small teacher-to-student ratio, students grow up to be independent thinkers.

It is our foremost goal to prepare students in every aspect so that they can develop the confidence necessary for success in their next stage of learning.
